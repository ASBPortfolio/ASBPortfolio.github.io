PCPNDT Monitoring Portal – Government of Andhra Pradesh

Link: https://pcpndt.ap.gov.in/

Web Application

Developed and maintained a government portal for enforcing the PCPNDT (Pre-Conception and Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques) Act in Andhra Pradesh. The system enables online registration and tracking of diagnostic centers, inspections, and complaint submissions to prevent illegal sex determination. Integrated public reporting features, digital case logs, and alert systems to support real-time monitoring and compliance.

Clinical Establishments Registration Portal – Government of Andhra Pradesh

Link: https://clinicalesttact.ap.gov.in/

Web Application

Developed and maintained a comprehensive digital platform for the registration and regulation of private allopathic medical care establishments across Andhra Pradesh, in compliance with the Andhra Pradesh Allopathic Private Medical Care Establishments (Registration and Regulation) Act, 2002. The portal facilitates online registration, renewal, and compliance monitoring for hospitals, clinics, diagnostic centers, and other healthcare facilities. Integrated features include user authentication, document management, payment processing, and real-time status tracking, streamlining the regulatory process and enhancing transparency in the healthcare sector.

Electronic Health Records (EHR) Portal – Government of Andhra Pradesh

Link: https://ehr.ap.gov.in/

Web and Mobile Application

Contributed to the development and maintenance of the Electronic Health Records (EHR) Portal for the Government of Andhra Pradesh, a pivotal initiative under the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM). The portal facilitates the digitization and integration of citizens' health records across various healthcare facilities, enabling seamless access to medical histories and enhancing clinical decision-making. Key features include the creation of Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA) numbers, interoperability of health data, and real-time tracking of patient information. This initiative positioned Andhra Pradesh as the first state to link over one crore health records to the ABDM, significantly advancing the state's digital health infrastructure.

NCDCD Portal – Government of Andhra Pradesh

Link: http://ncdcd.ap.gov.in/

Web and Mobile Application

Contributed to the development and maintenance of the NCDCD (Non-Communicable Diseases & Communicable Diseases) Portal for the Government of Andhra Pradesh. This platform supports the National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases, and Stroke (NPCDCS) by facilitating health promotion, opportunistic screening, capacity building, and surveillance of NCDs across the state. Key features include online registration of health facilities, patient data management, real-time monitoring of disease prevalence, and integration with district-level health systems to enhance early detection and treatment of NCDs.

National Leprosy Eradication Programme (NLEP) Portal – Government of Andhra Pradesh

Link: https://hmfw.ap.gov.in/nlep-program.aspx

Web and Mobile Application

This portal supports the implementation of the Government of India's NLEP in Andhra Pradesh, aiming to eliminate leprosy with a prevalence rate of less than 1 case per 10,000 population. It integrates leprosy services into general healthcare, enabling early case detection, household contact surveys, and treatment coordination with Accredited Social Health Activists (ASHAs). It also streamlines disability prevention, medical rehabilitation, real-time monitoring, and community awareness (IEC) campaigns to reduce stigma and improve health outcomes. The platform also supports monitoring of key performance indicators such as new case detection rates, prevalence rates, and the proportion of grade 2 disabilities among new cases .
